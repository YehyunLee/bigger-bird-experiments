Project Goal

Reduce Transformer self-attention from O(n²) to O(n) via content-aware discrete routing. Bigger Bird patches BART-base with three selection mechanisms: diversity-aware local top-k, submodular global selection, and biased random teleports.

May 2026 Fix Sprint — Key Findings

  • Old 6k benchmark used dynamic padding (~166 tokens) — sparse params weren't sparse at that length.
  • Old kernels did full O(n²) QKᵀ before masking; fixed to head-shared routing + efficient gathers.
  • Unfair pooling: patched models used AttnPool, baseline used first-token. Now all use last_hidden[:,0,:].
  • Post-fix: exp1 & exp4 ~3× faster than dense at 2048 tokens; exp3 at parity; exp2 still slower.
  • Next phase: long-context fixed-length training (512–4096 tokens, 6k–25k samples).
